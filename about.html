Further information regarding the upcoming festivals

Festivals will be going on year round and we look forward to seeing every fan who can come join us. There will be raffles in addition to great music. In the evening there will be behind the stage passes given away at each of the festivals at randome. All you have to do is give a small donation to the charity of the day. Each festival will be supporting a local charity to help those in need. Dont miss your chance at meeting your favorit band members!
