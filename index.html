Beach Volleyball

Beach Volleyball began in the early 1920s. Most accounts place the sport's origin in Santa Monica, California, where the first Volleyball courts were put up on the beach at the Playground (Santa Monica). Families played 6 vs 6. As time has passed the sport has evolved into an Olympic sport. In the Olympic's the teams are divided in to teams of 2 v 2.

Hazards of Volleyball

  1. Being an outdoor sport weather is a factor.
  2. Strained ankles,knees,fingers, and shoulders are common.
  3. Broken fingers, and noses are also common.
  4. Sun burn and the heated sand burning the souls of your feet.
  5. Tears like a strain but much more sever causing permanent damage.
